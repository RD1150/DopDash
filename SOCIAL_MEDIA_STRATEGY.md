## Engagement Strategy

### Community Building
- **Respond to all comments** within 24 hours
- **Engage with ADHD community** (follow, comment, share relevant content)
- **Host weekly Q&A** on Instagram Stories or Twitter Spaces
- **Feature user stories** and testimonials

### Growth Tactics
- **Collaborate with ADHD creators** for cross-promotion
- **Partner with mental health accounts** for reach
- **Run contests** (e.g., "Share your task paralysis story, win premium access")
- **Use trending sounds/formats** on TikTok/Instagram

### Conversion Focus
- **Every post links to landing page** (in bio or comments)
- **Clear CTAs** in captions ("Try free," "Join waitlist," "Learn more")
- **Track link clicks** and conversions by platform
- **A/B test messaging** to find what resonates
