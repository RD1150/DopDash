<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neumorphism (Soft UI) meets Zen Minimalism
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Softness**: Everything should feel touchable, soft, and non-threatening.
  2. **Calmness**: Low contrast, muted colors, no sharp edges.
  3. **Focus**: One primary action per screen, zero clutter.
  4. **Gentle Feedback**: Interactions should feel like a soft pulse or a gentle nod, not a loud ding.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Off-white / Soft Cream (#F5F5F7) or very light cool grey.
  - **Accents**: Muted Sage Green (Calm), Soft Periwinkle (Playful), Warm Sand (Matter-of-fact).
  - **Intent**: To reduce visual noise and anxiety. The screen should look like a piece of high-quality paper or a smooth stone.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Card-based Stack**: Screens feel like a stack of calming cards.
  - **Centered but Breathing**: Content is centered for focus, but with massive padding to let it breathe.
  - **Bottom-heavy Controls**: All interactive elements within thumb reach, grounding the experience.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Soft Shadows**: Neumorphic indentations for pressed states, soft extrusions for unpressed.
  - **Rounded Everything**: Super-ellipses for buttons and containers.
  - **Glows**: Subtle, breathing glows for rewards instead of confetti.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Tap & Hold**: For completing actions, a satisfying "fill" animation on hold feels more deliberate than a quick tap.
  - **Slide**: Slide to complete for bigger milestones.
  - **Fluid Transitions**: Screens dissolve into each other; no hard cuts.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slow & Smooth**: Easing curves should be long and gentle (ease-in-out).
  - **Breathing**: The "Start" button might slowly expand and contract like a breath.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: Rounded Sans-serif (e.g., Nunito or Quicksand) - friendly and approachable.
  - **Body**: Clean Sans-serif (e.g., Lato or Mulish) - highly readable but soft.
  - **Hierarchy**: Size and weight denote importance, but contrast is kept low (dark grey vs. black).
</idea>
</text>

  **Design Movement**: Brutalist Pop (Friendly Edition)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Clarity**: Big, bold text. No ambiguity.
  2. **Honesty**: Raw materials, simple shapes, direct language.
  3. **Energy**: High contrast but warm colors to spark a little dopamine without overwhelming.
  4. **Tactility**: Buttons look like physical buttons you want to smash.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Warm Beige or Pale Yellow (#FEF9E7).
  - **Accents**: Electric Blue, Sunset Orange, Vivid Purple.
  - **Intent**: To cut through the "fog" of executive dysfunction with clear, distinct visual signals.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen / Grid**: Big blocks of color separating content.
  - **Asymmetric Balance**: Text on one side, big icon/button on the other.
  - **Sticky Headers/Footers**: Anchoring the experience.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Thick Borders**: 2px-3px solid black borders on everything.
  - **Drop Shadows**: Hard, non-blurred drop shadows (retro web style).
  - **Emoji/Iconography**: Large, custom 3D-style icons or flat, bold glyphs.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Buttons depress visually (translate Y).
  - **Snappy**: Fast transitions, no lag.
  - **Confetti/Particles**: Physical-feeling particles for rewards.

  **Animation**:
  - **Bouncy**: Spring physics for interactions.
  - **Pop**: Elements pop in with a slight overshoot.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: Bold Display Serif (e.g., Cooper Black or similar chunky font) or a heavy Grotesque.
  - **Body**: Monospace (e.g., Space Mono) for a "tool-like" feel.
  - **Hierarchy**: Massive size differences.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Ethereal Gradient / Aurora
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Flow**: The background is alive and constantly moving (slowly).
  2. **Immersion**: The UI feels like a window into a calm, colorful world.
  3. **Light**: Elements are defined by light and transparency (Glassmorphism).
  4. **Uplifting**: Colors are chosen to boost mood subconsciously.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Deep, rich gradients (Midnight Blue to Purple, or Dawn Orange to Pink).
  - **Accents**: White (translucent) and bright gradients.
  - **Intent**: To create a "safe space" that feels magical and removed from daily stress.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Elements**: Content floats in glass panes over the moving background.
  - **Full Screen**: No hard boundaries, the background bleeds to edges.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Frosted Glass**: Background blur (backdrop-filter) for readability.
  - **Gradients**: Everywhere. Text gradients, border gradients.
  - **Orbs**: Glowing orbs moving in the background.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Ripple Effects**: Taps create ripples.
  - **Parallax**: Tilting the phone (simulated) moves layers.

  **Animation**:
  - **Drifting**: Background elements drift.
  - **Fade & Scale**: Elements enter by fading and scaling up gently.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display) for a touch of class/magic.
  - **Body**: Geometric Sans (e.g., Montserrat).
  - **Hierarchy**: Weight and opacity (100% vs 70% white).
</idea>
