# Dopamine Dasher Launch Posts

## 1. REDDIT - r/ADHD (Main Launch Post)

**Title:** I built Dopamine Dasher for my ADHD brain and I'm finally sharing it - no login, no guilt, just wins

**Post:**

I have ADHD and I'm tired of task apps that make me feel broken.

Every productivity app I've tried follows the same formula: big lists, overwhelming features, guilt when I don't finish. They're designed for neurotypical brains that can push through resistance. Mine doesn't work that way.

So I built **Dopamine Dasher** - a task app that actually gets how ADHD brains work.

**Here's what makes it different:**

- **Instant gratification** - Complete a task, see the celebration immediately. Your brain gets the dopamine hit it needs.
- **Micro-tasks only** - 2-5 minute wins. No 3-hour projects. Just achievable momentum.
- **Gamified rewards** - Streaks, badges, and visual progress. It feels like playing, not working.
- **Zero friction** - No login. No signup. No explanation walls. Just start.
- **ADHD-designed** - Built by someone with ADHD, for people with ADHD. No judgment. No shame.

**The honest part:** I built this because I was staring at my to-do list for 20 minutes doing nothing. Task paralysis is real. But when I broke things into tiny wins and celebrated each one? Everything changed.

It's live now at **dopaminedasher.com** (completely free to try).

I'm not trying to sell you anything. I just built the app I needed and thought you might need it too.

Try it. Tell me what breaks. Tell me what helps. This is just the beginning.
