# ConvertKit Setup Guide for Dopamine Dasher Email Capture

## Why ConvertKit?

- **Free tier:** Up to 1,000 subscribers (perfect for starting out)
- **Easy integration:** Simple forms and embeds
- **ADHD-friendly:** Clean, simple interface
- **Email sequences:** Automate launch announcements
- **No credit card required** for free tier

## Step 1: Create Your ConvertKit Account (5 minutes)

1. Go to **https://convertkit.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up Free"**
3. Enter your email (use your current email, not the old one!)
4. Create a password
5. Click **"Create Account"**
6. Verify your email address
7. **Done!** You now have a free ConvertKit account

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Form (10 minutes)

### **In ConvertKit Dashboard:**

1. Click **"Forms"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create a Form"**
3. Choose **"Inline Form"** or **"Modal Form"**
   - **Inline:** Sits on your page (recommended for Dopamine Dasher)
   - **Modal:** Pops up when visitor arrives

4. **Customize the form:**
   - **Headline:** "Get launch updates"
   - **Description:** "Be the first to know when Dopamine Dasher launches. No spam, just wins."
   - **Button text:** "Notify Me" or "Join the List"
   - **Color:** Match your Dopamine Dasher green theme

5. Click **"Create Form"**

---

## Step 3: Get Your Form Embed Code (5 minutes)

1. In your new form, click **"Embed"** or **"Share"**
2. Choose **"Embed on Website"**
3. Copy the **HTML embed code** (looks like `<script>...</script>`)
4. Save this code - you'll need it next

---

## Step 4: Add the Form to Your Landing Page (15 minutes)

### **Option A: Simple HTML Embed (Easiest)**

If your Dopamine Dasher landing page allows custom HTML:

1. Go to your landing page code
2. Find where you want the form to appear (probably after the CTA button)
3. Paste the ConvertKit embed code there
4. Save and test

### **Option B: Using Zapier (If you want to store emails elsewhere)**

If you want emails to go to Google Sheets, Mailchimp, or another service:

1. Go to **https://zapier.com**
2. Create a new Zap
3. **Trigger:** ConvertKit ‚Üí New Subscriber
4. **Action:** Google Sheets ‚Üí Add Row (or your preferred service)
5. Map the fields (email, name, etc.)
6. Test and activate

## Step 7: Monitor Your Subscribers (Ongoing)

1. **In ConvertKit Dashboard:**
   - Click **"Subscribers"** to see all emails collected
   - Click **"Sequences"** to see email performance
   - Monitor open rates and clicks

2. **What to look for:**
   - Open rate: Aim for 30%+ (good subject lines help)
   - Click rate: Aim for 5%+ (good CTAs help)
   - Unsubscribe rate: Keep below 1% (don't spam!)

## Troubleshooting

### **"Form isn't showing on my page"**
- Check that the embed code is in the right place
- Make sure it's not inside another form
- Check browser console for JavaScript errors
- Contact ConvertKit support

### **"Emails aren't being collected"**
- Test with a real email (not test@test.com)
- Check spam folder
- Verify form is properly embedded
- Check ConvertKit subscriber list

### **"I want to export my emails"**
- ConvertKit ‚Üí Subscribers ‚Üí Export as CSV
- Use Zapier to sync to Google Sheets automatically

### **"Can I use my own domain for emails?"**
- Not on free tier
- Upgrade to paid plan for custom domain
- Or use Zapier to forward emails elsewhere
