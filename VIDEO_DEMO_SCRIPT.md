## Production Notes

### Tone & Style
- **Energy Level:** High, fast-paced, optimistic
- **Music:** Upbeat, modern, electronic/indie vibe (no copyright issues)
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts, smooth transitions
- **Color Palette:** Lime green, teal, white background (brand colors)

### Key Elements to Highlight
1. **Problem Recognition** - Relatable ADHD struggle
2. **Speed** - "30 seconds" messaging
3. **Simplicity** - 3 questions, done
4. **Reward** - Confetti, celebration, dopamine hit
5. **Zero Friction** - No login, no credit card
6. **CTA** - Clear link to website

### Recommended Tools
- **Video Editor:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve
- **Music:** Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free YouTube Audio Library
- **Screen Recording:** ScreenFlow (Mac), OBS (Windows/Mac), or Camtasia
- **Animation:** After Effects for confetti and transitions

### Suggested Music
- Upbeat, modern electronic
- 120-130 BPM
- No lyrics (or minimal)
- Energetic but not overwhelming
- Examples: "Sunny Days" by Anno Domini Beats, "Uplifting" by Epidemic Sound

### Confetti Animation Specifications
- **Timing:** Trigger on task completion
- **Duration:** 2-3 seconds
- **Colors:** Green (#22c55e), Teal (#14b8a6), Orange (#f59e0b), Pink (#ec4899), Purple (#8b5cf6)
- **Particle Count:** 100-150 particles
- **Effect:** Burst from center, gravity-based fall
