## Paid Ad Strategy

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- **Audience:** People interested in ADHD, productivity, mental health
- **Budget:** $500-1000/week to start
- **Creatives:** Carousel ads, video ads, static image ads
- **Placements:** Feed, Stories, Reels
- **Objective:** Conversions (app downloads or email signups)

### Google Ads
- **Keywords:** "ADHD task app", "productivity for ADHD", "task paralysis", "ADHD productivity"
- **Budget:** $300-500/week to start
- **Ad Copy:** Focus on "no login", "free trial", "ADHD-friendly"
- **Landing Page:** dopaminedasher.com
- **Objective:** Clicks to website

### TikTok Ads
- **Audience:** 18-35, interested in ADHD, mental health, productivity
- **Budget:** $200-300/week to start
- **Creatives:** 15-30 second video ads (use demo video)
- **Placements:** TikTok Feed
- **Objective:** App installs or website clicks

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Audience:** Professionals interested in productivity, mental health
- **Budget:** $200-300/week to start
- **Creatives:** Thought leadership content, testimonials
- **Placements:** Feed, Sponsored InMail
- **Objective:** Website clicks, lead generation

## Performance Tracking

### Metrics to Monitor
- **CTR** (Click-Through Rate) - Target: 2-5%
- **CPC** (Cost Per Click) - Target: $0.50-$2.00
- **Conversion Rate** - Target: 5-15%
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) - Target: $5-$20
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend) - Target: 3:1 or higher

### Tools
- Google Analytics for website traffic
- Facebook Ads Manager for social ads
- Mixpanel or Amplitude for in-app analytics
- Spreadsheet for tracking across channels
